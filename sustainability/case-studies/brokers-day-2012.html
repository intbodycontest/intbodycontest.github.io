      Brokers Day 2012

      For the last four years, we've roped in our partner brokers to donate their day’s trading fees to Seeing is Believing, our global initiative aimed at tackling avoidable blindness.

      Biggest single fundraiser

      Broker Day has, since, become the biggest single fundraiser for Seeing is Believing. Last year’s Broker Day raised USD1.2 million, including our matching.

      Since its launch in 2009, Broker Day has raised enough to fully fund two three-year projects to provide surgical training to doctors, equip new eye-care wards and build community eye care clinics in communities which lack access to eye-care.

      Brokers day 2012

      To celebrate Broker Day, Nitin Gulabani, our Global Head of FX, Rates and Credits recently visited a project in Jakarta to show the impact our partner Helen Keller is able to make through the support of Seeing is Believing.
