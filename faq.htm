TIPS: International Body Building Contest MEMBERSHIPS
  • 2015 International Body Building Contest memberships $120 expire Dec 31, 2015
  • You can purchase International Body Building Contest card @ weigh-ins & height check & there is no late fee
  • You must show proof of International Body Building Contest membership before you can compete NO EXCEPTIONS
  • If you have not purchased your International Body Building Contest membership at least 4 weeks prior to the show
Wait until weigh-ins. You must show proof of International Body Building Contest purchase or buy another.
  • Cash or check is acceptable to purchase International Body Building Contest card
TIPS: REGISTRATION & WEIGH-INS
  • Read the registration form thoroughly as all the information you need is on there.
  • To avoid late fee’s post mark entry 2 weeks (14 Days) before the date of show
  • You may register @ weigh-ins with a late fee
  • All fee’s must be paid in cash
  • Competitors may purchase Pre-Judging & Finals tickets for a $5 discount per ticket
  • Get to registration early enough to fill out paperwork before Comp meeting starts
  • Mandatory check in Friday before the show. If you miss check-ins you will not complete
OVERALL WINNERS
  • Overall Division winners it is Mandatory to stay till end of competition for photos
(Bodybuilding, Physique, Fitness, Figure, Bikini & Wheelchair)
  • It is an Honor to win the Overall so make sure you stay for the photos
TICKET INFO
  • VIP ticket sales $50 include general admission for Pre-judging & Reserve seats for Finals
  • VIP ticket purchases are only available by calling Steve 801-687-6409
  • Finals tickets $25 in advance (cash only) or $30 cash @ Door
  • Pre-Judging tickets $15 in advance @ (weigh-ins only) $20 @ Door
  • If you purchase your ticket/wristbands in advance put them on and walk in the Venue
  • If you need to purchase tickets @ door you must wait in line
  • There is no difference in ticket prices for kids or seniors – if you use a seat you buy a ticket
Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Is this show a National Qualifier ?
A: Yes. All International Body Building Contest shows are National Qualifier’s.

Q: If you are competing You may register at weigh-in and height check from 3:00-7:00 pm Friday before the competition.

Q: How do I get tickets for the competitions ?
A: Go to the website and click on tickets for instructions on pre-sale and V.I.P. tickets.

Q: What time does the show start ?
A: All International Body Building Contest shows start at the same time:
Pre-Judging Doors open 8:30 am
Pre-Judging Starts 9:00 am
Finals Doors open 5:00 pm
Finals Start 6:00 pm
