    Breeze Mobile Banking

    Do more with your time. Use our Breeze Banking and Trade mobile apps.

    BookmarkPrint

    Download Breeze for free on your iPhone, iPad or Android phone

    Do more with your time and get quick and easy access to your accounts on the go with our Breeze mobile apps: Breeze and Breeze Trade.

     

    What you can do with Breeze

    • Check your balance and view your transactions
    • Pay bills, transfer money and bank cheques
    • Take charge of your finances and savings goals.

    Breeze is available for our customers in: 

    • China
    • Ghana
    • Hong Kong
    • India
    • Indonesia
    • Jersey
    • Malaysia
    • Nigeria
    • Pakistan
    • Singapore
    • South Korea
    • Taiwan
    • United Arab Emirates
     

    What you can do with Breeze Trade

    • Manage your stock portfolio on the go
    • Get real-time stock quotes
    • View market updates and review your portfolio.

    Breeze Trade is available for our customers in:

    • Hong Kong

    Breeze is available to download on iTunes, Google Play, and can be accessed on Blackberry phones through a mobile browser in Hong Kong and the UAE.

    Breeze Trade is available to download on iTunes, and can be accessed via Android and Blackberry phones through a mobile browser.
