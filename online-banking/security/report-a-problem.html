    Report a problem


    Take control of your online security

    We'll never request your account information and password over the phone, via email or SMS. We’ll also never direct you to a site to input your username and password. Your password and PIN are private to you – Never reveal them to anyone.

    If you think you may have disclosed your account details to a suspicious party, contact us immediately by calling the number on the back of your bank card or statement.

    Send us the scam email

    If you receive a suspicious email claiming to be from us, forward it with the attachments included to be.secure@sc.com, so we can try to track where it's coming from.

     

    Have you received money laundering funds?

    If you think you may have disclosed your bank account details to suspicious parties and have reason to believe they're using it for money laundering, contact us via the number on the back of your bank card or statement, and inform your local enforcement authority immediately.

    Alternatively, you can scroll down and select your country online banking webpage to contact our customer service officers.
