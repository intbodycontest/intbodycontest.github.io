    How we protect you

    See our security in action

    Our banking systems are managed by skilled experts and protected by the best security technology available. No matter where you go, you can manage your money online with confidence and security. Read on to find out what we are doing to protect your accounts when you bank online.

    Strong technology

    • Firewalls and encryption
    • Superior authentication
    • Automatic logouts

    Strong firewalls protect our banking systems from external threats and risks.

    All customer data that moves between Standard Chartered and our customers are protected with state-of-the-art 256-bit encryption. Even if data is captured during transit, advanced encryption ensures it will never be used.

    Superior authentication technologies ensure only you, the authorised user, can access your account.

    • You'll only have three login attempts before we temporarily disable your account to prevent fraudsters from guessing your password. To reactivate, call the number on the back of your bank card or statement.
    • Advanced Second Factor Authentication (2FA) requires you to key in your username and password, as well as a unique and time-sensitive One Time Password (OTP) sent to your registered mobile phone in the form of an SMS. Even if someone gets hold of your ID and PIN, they still can't access your account.
    • In some of our markets, we also require an additional password for a third level of authentication.

    Long periods of login inactivity may increase the risk of fraud and other issues.

    We'll log you out of your online and mobile banking session after 15 minutes of inactivity to prevent others from gaining access to your account.

    Secure systems

    • Advanced 24/7
      monitoring
    • Email and
      SMS alerts
    • Secure
      eStatements
    • Secure transfers
      and bill payments

    All systems and activity are monitored by our expert security team, proactively working to combat threats and address issues.

    We monitor threats and online activities round the clock to detect any fraud early and take preventive measures to ensure your accounts are safe.

    You can configure SMS and email alerts to stay informed when important account information changes or payments and transfers are made using your accounts.

    • Instant SMS for all transactions made using your accounts
    • Amendments to balance threshold/limit
    • Changes to account data

    To sign up for SMS alerts, log in to online banking and activate it.

    eStatements are easy to use and any reduction in paper is great for the environment too.

    • All eStatement data is encrypted during transition
    • eStatements are password protected, and they are only sent to authorised email addresses
    • SMS/Email alerts inform you when your eStatement is ready

    Visit your country site and sign up for eStatements today.

    Additional alerts and authentication enhance the security of your online and mobile banking activities.

    • Second Factor Authentication (2FA) is required if you wish to add new payees, complete a bill payment or perform a fund transfer.
    • SMS/Email alerts inform you when a new payee has been added.
    • You can set daily transfer limits for your accounts.
