      Helping companies trade, globally

       

      For over 150 years we’ve been keeping international businesses moving. We work with clients to raise the funds they need, in the currencies they need to use, to trade in goods and services across the globe – allowing businesses to work, and economies to thrive.

      One of the ways we’ve done this is by being at the forefront of London’s development as a centre for trade in the Chinese Renmimbi (RMB) currency. Our recent issuance of RMB Euro Commercial Papers created a new way of raising RMB liquidity to support our clients’ trade in goods and services across the globe. All this supports businesses and the local economies which rely on them.

      Throughout our history, we’ve been committed to facilitating international trade for our clients. By offering clients the opportunity to capitalise on the internationalisation of the RMB, we continue to support economic growth, helping businesses go from strength to strength.
