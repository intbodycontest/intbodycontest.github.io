    Diversity and inclusion

    Our approach to diversity and inclusion is fundamental to who we are as an organisation, and a key aspect of our brand promise, Here for good.

    As an international bank, we have a naturally diverse workforce. We employ more than 90,000 people − nearly half of whom are women − representing 133 nationalities worldwide. This provides us with a strong competitive advantage, enabling us to understand better the needs of those who bank with us.
