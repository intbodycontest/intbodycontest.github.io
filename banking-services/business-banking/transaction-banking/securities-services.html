      Securities services

      We've been meeting securities industry participants' needs across Asia, Africa and the Middle East for over 150 years, serving a discerning client base that comprises leading North American, European and Asian institutions.

      We count among our clients the world's largest global custodians, broker-dealers, fund managers and institutional investors.

      As one of the world's leading international banks, we bring to you a wealth of diverse experience and standards combined with our local market expertise and banking infrastructure.

      Securities services aims to provide securities industry participants with tailored solutions to meet your unique needs in the securities services domain across our key markets in Asia, Africa and the Middle East.

      Our current extended sub-custody network covers:

      • Custody capability in-country for 40 markets across Asia, Africa and Middle East
      • Single-window service with access to domestic custody expertise
      • Best-in-class asset servicing platform and reporting
      • Advanced custody platform allowing event-driven processing, aggressive instruction deadlines, live transaction updates and intra-day position information

      In addition to providing custody and clearing services, we also offer full fund services in 21 domestic onshore markets across Asia, Africa and the Middle East and International fund services for UCITS via Luxembourg. This will provide you with full valuation services and a wide spectrum of customised reports on your portfolio.

      This service is specifically developed to offer you the flexibility of extensive reporting facilities and the ability to handle a wide variety of instruments.

      In addition to the above, we have worked with broker dealers to meet specific requirements for back office outsourcing, leveraging on the expertise residing within us in local market settlements.

      With our extensive network and comprehensive range of commercial banking solutions, we offer clients the convenience of dealing with a single provider for all their banking requirements.

      Our strengths lie in our:

      • Advanced information systems
      • In-depth local market expertise
      • Integrated and flexible core services
      • Strong client focus
      • Total quality management

      Together, these strengths enable us to give our clients an unsurpassed level of support and service.
