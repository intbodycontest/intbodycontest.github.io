Our operations are highly automated to ensure that your transactions are completed reliably, efficiently and securely.

      FedBen®

      For most U.S. banks, Fedwire®* is the primary channel for making international payments. Our FedBen® service gives your bank the ability to leverage our unmatched international payment capability by simply sending a Fedwire® to Standard Chartered. FedBen® is the quick and cost-effective way to easily effect payments directly to beneficiary banks’ correspondents overseas.

      Through the FedBen® service, the US bank can send international payments only through Standard Chartered via Fedwire®, thereby gaining better control over payment delivery, improving customer service, and generating new income.  This innovative solution pioneered by us seamlessly integrates into your standard payment processing environment.  One of the key features of our FedBen® service is that it will unlock the full revenue potential of international wires for your organisation while creating operational efficiencies by removing the burden of routing international wire transfers. 

      FedBen® gives you a “business as usual” solution to international USD transfers.  You simply open a FedBen® account with us.  Every time you send us a FedBen® eligible payment your account will be credited. Our FedBen® service will apply a beneficiary deduction and complete the international transfer by debiting your account in real time.

      *Fedwire® is a registered service mark of the Federal Reserve Banks.

      Euro/Sterling clearing

      Euro/Sterling clearing

      We're able to provide Euro products and clearing services in London, including inter-bank and commercial payments, as well as trade reimbursements. We work particularly closely with financial institutions in the emerging markets paying into Europe, financial institutions in Europe paying across Asia, and financial institutions in the Americas paying into Europe. Whatever your profile, you can be rest assured our Euro services, with its comprehensive features, will provide you with quality and consistency.

      In Hong Kong, we are the sole settlement institution of the local Euro clearing system. Standard Chartered Bank (Hong Kong) Ltd provides intra-day Euro liquidity for participating banks by means of intra-day overdraft facilities and intra-day sale and repurchase agreements (“repos”). Intra-day repos are supported by eligible papers (Hong Kong dollar exchange fund bills/notes and certificates of deposit issued by Standard Chartered).

      Disclosure Framework for Euro Real Time Gross Settlement (RTGS) System
      The Committee on Payment and Settlement Systems (CPSS) of the Bank for International Settlements and the International Organization of Securities Commissions (IOSCO) have jointly issued a disclosure framework and assessment methodology for their principles for financial market infrastructures (PFMIs), which is the new international standards for financial market infrastructures (FMIs).

      The disclosure framework (DF) is intended to promote consistent and comprehensive public disclosure by FMIs in line with the requirements of the PFMIs. The EUR RTGS system is subject to the DF, and the disclosure report of EUR RTGS system is completed according to the disclosure template outlined in the DF.

      TARGET Access
      We participate in TARGET2 via direct clearing connection. TARGET2 has common operating times throughout the European Union for customer and inter-bank payments.

      EBA Membership
      The Euro Banking Association (EBA) operates the Euro1 Clearing System, which works on an end-of-day net settlement basis. We have been a clearing member of the EBA since its launch, and is able to make Euro payments via the Euro1 Clearing System for transactions of any value.
