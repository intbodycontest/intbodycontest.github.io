    Advisory & financing solutions

    We combine advisory and financing solutions with industry expertise to provide real value for our clients.

    Our teams work synergistically, leveraging each other’s geographic, industry and structuring expertise. Our dedicated globally integrated industry teams – in Mining and Metals, Oil and Gas, Clean Energy, and Telecommunications, Media, Technology – work seamlessly with our Mergers and Acquisitions Advisory and Financing teams to provide world class bespoke solutions for our clients.

    We help clients identify and evaluate potential targets, sellers or investors, we also provide advice for structuring and providing complex limited recourse financings for energy or infrastructure projects and acquisitions.

    We are also able to provide export finance for the import of capital goods with support of export credit agencies.


    Industry and geographic expertise


    Our industry and geographic expertise have been strengthened through selective acquisitions.

    In August 2011, we acquired Gryphon Partners, an advisory firm focused on the mining and metals sector with operations across Australia and Canada. The new business has enabled us to provide our clients with deeper expertise in advising on complex mergers, takeovers, restructures and financings in the sector. We also have an Oil and Gas team that is a global leader in corporate finance and advisory for the sector. The team was strengthened in 2007 with the acquisition of pre-eminent oil and gas advisory boutique Harrison Lovegrove & Co.

    In 2009, we completed the acquisition of First Africa, a leading pan-African Mergers and Acquisitions advisory firm, bolstering our ability to provide strategic advice in relation to Mergers and Acquisitions  activity between pan-African companies and to corporates investing in Africa. 

    For more information, please contact your Relationship Manager.
