      Wealth Management

      Our programme is designed to set you up for success in your first full-time role. What you do and where you go across the breadth of our business is up to you.

      What is Wealth Management?

      Our Wealth Management teams use a comprehensive range of products and solutions to help our clients grow and protect their wealth. To do this, we also work with partners such as asset management firms and insurance companies.

      • Our Clients - Our Wealth Management services cater to the needs of different types of clients - from individuals to small business and corporate clients.

      • Our Teams - We also have teams of Investments Advisors, Insurance Specialists and Treasury Specialists, in-country who interact with clients and Relationship Managers to provide expert insights on global markets and advice to clients.

      What would a year in our International Graduate Programme look like?

      • Induction - This is where you'll get to know how Standard Chartered operates in your location. We'll set you up to get started.
      • Month 1: in Rotation in Platform or Operations - You’ll learn the fundamentals of how client transactions are processed and how we service our clients.
      • Month 2-3: Induction in Shanghai (5 Weeks) - Get ready for an incredible experience. This is your ultimate induction to the Bank. You’ll make connections from around the world and learn everything you need to be successful in your rotations.
      • Month 4-6: Rotation in Risk - You'll learn about our approach to risk and how we manage it effectively.
      • Month 7-12: 2x Rotations in Front Office - Depending on where you join us in the world, your final six months will see you complete two hands-on business rotations.
      • Deployment - Transition to your first full-time role.

      More about the programme:

      What sort of work will you be involved in?show/hide

      Banking is an exciting and dynamic industry. In your first year you will be given the broadest possible exposure to our business. These are some of the projects and experiences you can expect:

      • Working in a product team, working in Investments, Insurance or Capital Markets. You will participate in assessing which mutual funds or insurance solutions are appropriate for our clients.
      • The Investment Advisory team monitor the global markets and provide market outlook for our Relationship teams and clients. You could assist a senior colleague in the production of market reports and experience how these reports help clients make more informed decisions on their investment portfolios.
      • Our Wealth Client Proposition team looks at how we package, communicate and present our wealth management proposition to clients. Here, you will have the opportunity to use analytics to understand client behaviours and formulate a contact plan to reach out to clients with appropriate solutions or propositions.

      What are the application requirements?show/hide

      To apply for this stream you must have the following:

      • An undergraduate degree.
      • The legal right to work in the country that you are applying to.
      • Business level English (the ability to speak more than one language will be highly regarded).

      Advice from some Graduate Alumnishow/hide

      Okpako Biakolo - Product Head, Bancassurance - Botswana

      “Designing appropriate products for our customer base has been really exciting.”

      I studied Mathematics at university and after graduating did an MBA. Being exposed to this type of work, and enjoying what I was learning, helped me realise my passions. 

      I thought that the financial industry would be a great fit for my career, but didn’t know I would necessarily work in a Bank. Banking has been through a bit of a tough time and I wanted to be part of the solution rather part of the problem.

      I asked the career experts at my university for advice as well as speaking to people in the industry to give me an inside view. Standard Chartered has a strong international focus and building a network all over the world was something that really appealed to me.

      I chose the Wealth Management stream. I’ve had the opportunity to work on the expansion of the retail part of the Bancassurance to increase penetration of the products. Working with teams to design appropriate products for our customer base has been really exciting.

      Talha Ahmad Ansari - Product Manager, Treasury Products - Pakistan

      “The IG programme is the top graduate programme in the industry.”

      I studied Accounting & Finance and Business Administration internationally, in the UK. I was always interested in the financial industry and often found myself reading different books and researching the global financial industry.

      I come from a family of industrialists, however my Father was the odd one out who decided to venture outside of the family business and joined a bank for the first 10 years of his career. His experiences played a big role in influencing me, as a result by high school I was sure I wanted to be in the finance industry as well.
       
      Standard Chartered is the largest and oldest bank in Pakistan. There is a certain prestige in being a part of this Bank, and as it is said locally, a Standard Chartered banker is always distinguishable from other bankers.
       
      The International Graduate Programme is the top graduate programme in the industry, therefore the need to be a part of it was a no brainer for me. I’ve had such broad experience and I continue to learn every day.
