      When you’re trying to make a decision about your career there’s a lot to think about. It can be overwhelming, sometimes confusing. That’s why we’ve created BankLab.

      What is BankLab?

      BankLab is a in-office experience designed to help you experiment with your career, and show you what a career in banking is really like. And when we say ‘show’, we don’t mean sitting you through a long presentation. We mean getting you involved in the kind of work that we do every day.

      You’ll be speaking to our people, working on challenges, getting to understand yourself better and experiencing our culture.

      Why should I go?

      There are lots of reasons why you should come to BankLab, here are just a few of them:

      • Visit our offices
      • Experience our culture
      • Speak to our people find out first hand what it’s like to work for us
      • Find out where your strengths fit best in
      • Explore our Graduate programmes

      If you’re interested in a career in banking, but have questions about getting into the industry then BankLab can help. In fact, even if you’ve never considered a career in banking, BankLab will show you the possibilities.

      To find out more and sign up for a BankLab in your country, take a look at our events calendar.
