    Fatima Al Zadjali

    Customer Value Proposition Manager - Consumer Banking

    You

    I joined Standard Chartered following a degree in International Tourism Management with Human Resources from the Middlesex University.

    Your journey

    I’ve always had a passion for banking so I decided to pursue this as a career. The graduate programme gave me the chance to rotate around different departments and get a feel for what happens and how they work. It really made a difference to experience it firsthand and find out what was the best fit for me.

    As an International Graduate you will receive tremendous support to help you perform and succeed. There are a lot of learning and development opportunities and my mentor in Dubai was extremely encouraging. We met regularly and he would patiently answer all my questions and help me fit in with the organisation. To have someone who I could talk to and who understood what I was going through made my journey that much easier.

    Your future

    I have had many opportunities to meet with senior managers in the Bank to better understand what they do and their vision for the future. This was very helpful when it came to deciding which area of the business I wanted to join upon completing the programme. I’ve joined the Employee Banking team as a Product Manager and I’m really enjoying the role, I’m also excited about what my future at Standard Chartered holds.

    What’s next?
