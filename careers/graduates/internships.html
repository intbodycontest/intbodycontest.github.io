      Internships

       

      Kick-start your career by exploring what you’re great at, while gaining valuable banking experience.

      Our Internship programme provides final year university students with the opportunity to gain firsthand experience in some of the world’s most dynamic markets in Asia, Africa and the Middle East. Explore what you enjoy doing most and discover how you can contribute to the way the world banks.

      About the programme

      Work that is fast-paced, challenging and dynamic is what our Internship programme is all about. Our internships are typically 2 months in length and give you the opportunity to see what a career in a truly global bank feels like.

      As an intern, you won’t simply be standing on the sidelines. You’ll be immersed in a hands-on learning experience by contributing to key projects aligned to our business, while building your professional network with some of the best and brightest in the banking industry.

      Along the way, you’ll develop valuable skills and experience, and benefit from close support and guidance from our senior staff, that will enable you to make the most of your time with us and develop an edge that you can take into your career after university.

      At the end of the programme, high performing interns may secure a full-time place on our Graduate Programme when you complete your studies.

      Internship streams

      Transaction Bankingshow/hide

      Transaction Banking supports the buying and selling of goods and services across borders, providing products and international networks to facilitate trade.  It aligns sales, product management and client service around our client segments – an approach that makes us unique in the industry.  We focus on understanding our client’s needs and providing best-in-class working capital solutions across the entire suite of transactional products and services. This intense focus on the client is how we build deep, durable relationships. We help our clients manage their working capital and obtain critical financing to keep the flow of goods and services moving, helping investors to access markets in Asia, Africa and the Middle East.

      Wealth Managementshow/hide

      At Wealth Management we work closely with the client segments providing wealth management and insurance solutions to our clients.  We deliver a comprehensive range of investment, treasury and insurance products to help clients grow and protect their wealth.  In addition, supported by our partners in the functions, we are responsible for all product and provide due diligence, end-to-end sales and governance processes and wealth management platforms. Our Wealth Management services cater to the needs of different types of clients - from individuals to small business and corporate clients.

      Financial Marketsshow/hide

      The Financial Markets teams have expertise and deep local market knowledge to deliver a variety of risk management, financing and investment solutions to our clients.  Offering a full suite of fixed income, currencies, commodities, equities and capital markets solutions, the Financial Markets business comprises Sales, Trading, Capital Markets, Asset and Liability Management, Structuring and Research.

      Commercial Clientsshow/hide

      At Commercial Clients we Help mid-sized local companies grow, especially as they expand across borders. Commercial Clients provides solutions for companies during one of their most exciting phases; the critical growth transition from a small business to an entry level corporate. These can be entrepreneurs and local businesses to mature or fast growing middle market companies, and most will have expanded internationally.  Our team helps them to facilitate trade with their buyers and suppliers, manage their working capital and transactional needs, assist them in managing volatility (currency, commodity prices, interest rates), as well as providing markets and corporate finance solutions when their needs become more complex.

      Corporate & Institutional Clients and Corporate Financeshow/hide

      Corporate & Institutional Clients (C&IC) -  Services the banking needs of our global and large scale enterprise, financial institutions and government clients. Getting to know our biggest clients, their businesses and challenges is the role of the C&IC team. They provide global expertise and local support, and are responsible for bringing together the right people to offer the right solutions for our clients.

      Corporate Finance - Helps clients build their businesses through financial and strategic advice. The team offers a range of products including Mergers and Acquisitions, Leveraged Finance, Equity Corporate Finance, Project and Export Finance, Structured Trade Finance, Principal Finance and Structured Finance.

      Group Technology and Operationsshow/hide

      Our Group Technology & Operations (GTO) business makes the delivery of our banking operations possible across our global footprint, we provide processes, technology and project management skills to support the Bank’s 95,000 employees. Our employees use 1,000 different systems, and require user support across 85 countries. GTO is the engine of the Bank.
      GTO is key to the Bank’s success and provides the foundation for our operation
      it is powered by over 27,000 employees and is the second largest business across the Bank globally.The GTO business is how the bank runs; how people work, how projects come into being and how initiatives take shape.GTO is organised around an Engineer, Build and Run model that brings technical and operational design together. The business is constantly evolving and drawing inspiration from the best-in-class product manufacturers.
