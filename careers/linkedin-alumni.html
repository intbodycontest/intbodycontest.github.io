      Alumni on LinkedIn

      Join the Standard Chartered LinkedIn group, exclusively for current and former staff, to be part of the conversation and build your network.

      BookmarkPrint

       

      Why join

      If you were asked to summarise in three words what Standard Chartered’s culture is all about, what would you say? Relationships, relationships, relationships?

      The group is a great way to build relationships, share knowledge and experiences, and get the latest industry insights. You can also connect with your former colleagues at the Bank and keep those connections no matter where people move in their careers.

      About the group

      Originally set up by our Singaporean colleagues Lawrence Ng and Chris Knight in 2007, the group has grown to over 10,000 members globally.

      To help ensure members receive the latest content, the group is now run by our Alumni team, with Lawrence and Chris still helping with its management.

      Next time you’re on LinkedIn, send a request to join the Standard Chartered Bank group.
