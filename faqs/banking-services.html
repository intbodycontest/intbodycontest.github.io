    Frequently Asked Questions - Investor Relations                                                                            

    Where is Standard Chartered stock traded?show/hide
    Standard Chartered ordinary shares are listed on the London Stock Exchange and Hong Kong Stock Exchange. Our Indian Depository Receipts (IDRs) are listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange and National Stock Exchange of India. Ten IDRs represent one ordinary share.

    How do I buy and trade shares?show/hide
    You can buy and sell our ordinary shares through a financial adviser or stockbroker, a bank or building society, or share shops that offer a similar service. The commission charges for buying and selling shares will vary depending on the organisation.

    Where can I get shareholder information online?show/hide

    You can get details of your current holdings, dividends and other shareholder information through our registrars' websites. You can also register to receive email e-mail updates when shareholder documents are available instead of receiving printed documents.

    UK registrar: http://www.investorcentre.co.uk/

    India registrar: http://karisma.karvy.com/html/index2.htm

    Why do I only receive an annual review and summary financial statement instead of the full statutory report and accounts?show/hide

    Most of our shareholders opt to receive the annual review and we make every effort to ensure that it contains the right balance of information. Being a review, it doesn't contain as much technical information as the full report and accounts.

    If you would like to receive the full report and accounts, please contact the Shareholder Helpline on +44 (0)870 702 0138 (UK) or +852 2862 8628 (Hong Kong).

    Is it possible to receive documents from Standard Chartered in Chinese?show/hide

    Chinese documents are available to shareholders who are registered on the Hong Kong register. Please contact Computershare Hong Kong Investor Services Limited via www.computershare.com/hk/investors.

    Where can I find stock exchange announcements released by Standard Chartered?show/hide
    You can find Stock Exchange announcements, released on the London Stock Exchange, on our website: http://investors.standardchartered.com/releases.cfm
    How many shares does Standard Chartered have in issue?show/hide

    We issue a 'Total Voting Rights' announcement once a month which details the number of ordinary and preference shares in issue. Stock Exchange announcements can be viewed on our website at: http://investors.standardchartered.com/releases.cfm

    How do I know when Standard Chartered will report its next Financial Results?show/hide

    All upcoming announcements and events are listed on our Financial Calendar.

    What is Standard Chartered's official accounting currency?show/hide

    Our official accounting currency is US dollars. This is because most of our business is carried out in US dollars or currencies linked to the US dollar.
