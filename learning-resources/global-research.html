    Global research

    We deliver differentiated research to the people and companies driving investment, trade and the creation of wealth across Asia, Africa and the Middle East.

    Standard Chartered Global Research delivers incisive and high-conviction research analysis and views that cover macroeconomics, fixed income, commodities and equities.

    Our team

    Our award-winning team consists of over 120 analysts, economists and strategists based in 14 locations around the globe. This on-the-ground presence – particularly in Asia, Africa and the Middle East – allows us to study these dynamic regions in depth and gives our research tremendous authority through the team’s local insight and networks.

    The team continuously interacts with the Bank's external clients through frequent presentations and the production of a range of high-quality publications. Our very best ideas are captured through our branded product suite of SCout, ACT and OTG.*

    Delivering relevant and differentiated content to our clients is central to our business aspiration. This in turn will help build trusted, long term relationships that support the Bank’s ambition to deliver sustainable growth.

    Visit our Global Research site. To get access, please contact your Relationship Manager.

    *SCout offers Strategic, Collaborative, Original ideas on Universal and Thematic opportunities, ACT provides trade ideas that are Actionable, high in Conviction and Timely, and OTG is Standard Chartered’s premium research product written by analysts On The Ground.
